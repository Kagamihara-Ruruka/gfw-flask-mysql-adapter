## Vostok 1 mission radio

- Files: `vostok1-gagarin-mission-radio.wav` (archival source) and `vostok1-gagarin-mission-radio-10s.mp3` (10-second browser playback excerpt).
- Source: [RadioScanner historical recording archive](https://www.radioscanner.ru/files/records/file2016/)
- Description: Russian-language communication between Yuri Gagarin and mission control during the Vostok 1 flight.
- Processing: the browser excerpt is trimmed from the authentic recording and transcoded from GSM-MS WAV to mono MP3; no synthetic speech is used.
- Note: retained as a cited historical recording; publication rights are not asserted by this repository.
